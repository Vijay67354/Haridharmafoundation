Cow Healthcare

Cow Healthcare, also known as a cow-calf operation, is a facility where cows and their calves are cared for by trained professionals. This type of daycare is essential for farmers who want to ensure the health and wellbeing of their livestock. The cows and their calves are kept in separate areas, with the calves being fed a special milk formula and the cows being provided with nutritious food and clean water.
